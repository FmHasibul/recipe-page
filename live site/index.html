Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy quick dish filled with perfection. Perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation :5 minutes

  • Cooking :5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs

  • Salt, to taste

  • Pepper, to taste

  • 1 tablespoon of butter or optional

  • Optional things cheese. diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instraction

  1. Beat the eggs:in a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper intil they are well mixed, You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a tluffier texture

  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pasn over medium heat and add butter or oil.

  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edge but are still slightly runny to the middle. sprinkle your choosen fillings over one half of omelette.

  5. Fold and serve: As the olmlette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings in the midddle, let it cook for another minute or two , then slide onto the plate.

  6. Enjoy: Serve hat, with additional salt and pepper if neened
